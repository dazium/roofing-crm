# RTR CRM Product Blueprint

## Brand
- **Company:** Rooftop Renovators
- **Short name:** RTR
- **Type:** single-company roofing CRM
- **Style:** premium dark contractor aesthetic with orange highlights

## Primary users
1. Admin / office
2. Sales reps
3. Crew leads / field crews

## Surfaces
### 1) Web CRM
- dashboard
- leads
- customers
- estimates
- jobs
- invoices
- reporting

### 2) Mobile field app
- my jobs
- my leads
- photo capture/upload
- notes/checklists
- job status updates

### 3) Public marketing site (next phase)
- homepage
- quote request
- services
- reviews
- contact

## MVP modules
### Leads
- capture and assign leads
- source tracking
- inspection booking
- notes
- lead-to-customer conversion

### Estimates
- line-item quotes
- taxes
- validity date
- sent/approved/rejected states

### Jobs
- schedule crews
- track status
- attach notes and photos
- tie jobs to approved estimates

### Billing
- invoices
- partial payments
- overdue visibility

### Media
- before/damage/progress/after photos

## Near-term backlog
- Supabase auth + row-level security
- quote PDF generation
- mobile camera and storage upload
- customer e-sign approval
- dashboard charts from live data
- SMS/email reminders
