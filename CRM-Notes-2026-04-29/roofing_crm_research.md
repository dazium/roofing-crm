# Roofing CRM Research Findings

## Key Features from iRoofer and Roofr

### Core CRM Features
1. **Customer Database Management**
   - Store contact details, phone numbers, emails
   - Track customer communication history
   - Import/export customer lists
   - Share customer database with team

2. **Project Management**
   - Job pipeline with drag-and-drop status updates
   - Real-time project tracking and updates
   - Job scheduling and timeline management
   - Task assignment and automation
   - Material tracking and ordering

3. **Estimates & Proposals**
   - Automated estimate generation
   - Line-item pricing and customization
   - Digital proposal presentation
   - Signature capture
   - Material catalogs integration

4. **Field Operations**
   - Mobile-first interface for on-site use
   - Photo capture and tagging
   - Digital roof measurements (CAD-like)
   - Document storage and access
   - Offline capability

5. **Scheduling & Calendar**
   - Appointment scheduling
   - Job calendar with team visibility
   - Automated reminders
   - Calendar sync capabilities

6. **Analytics & Reporting**
   - Sales pipeline visibility
   - Lead quality tracking
   - Revenue analytics
   - Team performance metrics
   - Close rate tracking

7. **Integration Features**
   - Material supplier ordering
   - Payment processing
   - Invoice generation
   - Automated workflows
   - Document management

## Design Insights
- Both apps use clean, modern interfaces
- Mobile responsiveness is critical (field use)
- Visual job pipeline/kanban boards are essential
- Real-time updates and notifications are important
- Emphasis on reducing paperwork and manual data entry

## Workflow Priorities for Roofing Contractors
1. Quick lead capture and management
2. Fast estimate generation
3. Job scheduling efficiency
4. Photo documentation
5. Team coordination
6. Payment tracking
7. Material ordering
